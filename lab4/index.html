How To Take the Bus from UCSC to Trader Joe's

  1. Make sure you have your student id and mask.
  2. Take any of the metro busses except the 22 to downtown. Make sure the bus doesn't say "LOOP".
  3. Get off at the Metro Station, which is the very last stop.
  4. Go north up on Front St. until you arrive at Front St. and Soquel Ave.
  5. You made it! Have fun shopping!
